Shipping container home

Shipping container homes are an economical, quick, and eco-friendly way of construction that repurposes used containers to create residential or commercial spaces. They are highly durable, fast to build, but require insulation and may have limited space.

The cost of such a container home can vary widely depending on several factors, such as size, the number of containers, additional features, and location. Here are approximate prices:

- Single-container homes: from $25,000 to $80,000.

- Multi-container homes: from $80,000 to $250,000 or more.

- Average cost per square foot: from $150 to $350.

Prices can increase if you choose more containers or additional features like high-quality interior finishes, kitchen, bathroom, and others.
